About Us

Terrified Interactive is a game development and engine-focused studio built to merge immersive gameplay systems with a bold visual identity.

Terrified Interactive began as a solo vision to create survival experiences with realistic physics, gore systems, and immersive combat mechanics. Today, the studio focuses on building modular engine technology that supports rich movement, AI, and environmental systems with cinematic presentation.

We specialize in creating reusable gameplay systems, polished motion layers, and scalable tech that helps developers turn ideas into immersive worlds without losing the feeling of pressure and intensity.
